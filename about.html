About

    Wave The Way is an arcade-style game in which the player controls the Spaceships,
    which moves persistently to the right. The player is tasked with navigating Spaceship
    through randomly generated obstacles that have gaps placed at random heights. Spaceship
    automatically descends and only ascends when the player taps the touchscreen.
    Each successful pass through the circle awards the player one point.
    Colliding with any obstacle or the ground ends the gameplay.
